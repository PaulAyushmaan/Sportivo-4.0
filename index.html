about us

Sports Festival

Techno Engineering College Banipur's annual sports day is a vibrant celebration of athleticism, teamwork, and spirit. Students showcase their prowess in a variety of sports and athletic competitions, fostering a sense of healthy competition and camaraderie. The event is marked by thrilling matches, energetic performances, and a grand opening and closing ceremony.

It serves as a platform to recognize and applaud the talents of the college's sports enthusiasts, creating memorable moments of unity and sportsmanship.
